Methamphetamine (Yaba/Ice)

A Dangerous Path to Destruction. Choose Health, Not Meth.

Catastrophic Brain Damage

Stimulant abuse can cause blood vessels to rupture, leading to severe intracerebral hemorrhage (bleeding in the brain). This can result in permanent brain damage, stroke, or death.

Severe Legal Consequences

In Thailand, drug offenses carry harsh penalties. A conviction for possession or trafficking can lead to long prison sentences, destroying future opportunities and impacting your entire life.

Cannabis (Ganja)

Your Brain is Developing. Protect Its Potential.

Alters Adolescent Brain Structure

The adolescent brain is still maturing. Cannabis use can interfere with this critical process, altering the development of pathways responsible for memory, learning, and emotional regulation.

Increased Risk of Psychosis

Studies show a strong link between adolescent cannabis use and an increased risk of developing serious mental health disorders, including anxiety, depression, and schizophrenia-like psychosis.

Kratom (4x100 Cocktail)

A Dangerous Mix. Don't Get Caught in the Cocktail.

The '4x100' Cocktail Menace

This dangerous mixture combines brewed kratom leaves with cough syrup and other substances. The resulting concoction is highly unpredictable and has led to numerous hospitalizations and deaths.

Leads to Opioid-like Addiction

At high doses, kratom binds to the same receptors as opioids. This creates a strong potential for physical dependence and addiction, with painful withdrawal symptoms similar to those of other opioids.

E-Cigarettes (Vaping)

Don't Be Deceived. Vaping is Not Harmless.

Vaping is Not Permitted

E-cigarettes are illegal in Thailand and often included in smoking bans globally. They are recognized as a public health risk, not a safe alternative to traditional cigarettes.

Causes Severe Lung Damage

The aerosol from vapes is not harmless "water vapor." It contains a toxic mix of chemicals and fine particles that can cause inflammation and irreversible damage to the lungs, similar to smoking.
